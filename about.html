About Us

We are a team of nature lovers and fishing enthusiasts committed to creating unforgettable fishing experiences in Rwanda. Our mission is to combine adventure with education by teaching sustainable fishing practices and fostering a deep appreciation for aquatic ecosystems. Since 2020, we have guided countless individuals through the beauty of Rwanda's lakes and rivers, helping both beginners and experts enjoy the art of fishing while preserving nature for future generations.
